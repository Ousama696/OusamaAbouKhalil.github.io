          My first project

          My project represents a method to detect leakage of gases. It's made through a series of connections and edited by a code to control the behavior of the sensor. It aims to help industries from catastrophes, which might occur in case of any leakage. It's practical, low cost, and most importantly, alarming. For example, as what happened in France, to be specific in Toulouse in the AZF industry after a huge fire occurred leading to an explosion after the chemical reaction between the fire and the ammonium nitrate, which led to loss of lives and money. Using this sensor can alert us early and maybe save the situation. In addition, at the 4th of August in Beirut, when hundreds died, and thousands lost their accommodations. If we had a gas leakage detector, what happened wouldn't have happened.
